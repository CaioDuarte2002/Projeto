BEST STATES FOR TOURISM!

RIO DE JANEIRO

Rio de Janeiro is a state in southeastern Brazil. Its capital, with the same name, is known for its beaches, the Sugar Loaf Mountain and the statue of Christ the Redeemer on top of Corcovado Hill. Architect Oscar Niemeyer's landmark, the Museum of Contemporary Art of Niterói, is on the opposite side of the Guanabara Bay in Niterói. Along the state's Atlantic coast is Ilha Grande, a wooded island surrounded by beaches, and the colonial city of Paraty.

SAO PAULO

São Paulo is one of the 27 federative units of Brazil. It is located in the Southeast Region and is bordered by the states of Minas Gerais to the north and northeast, Paraná to the south, Rio de Janeiro to the east and Mato Grosso do Sul to the west, in addition to the Atlantic Ocean to the southeast.

BAHIA

Bahia is a state in northeastern Brazil with landscapes ranging from the tropical coast to the arid Sertão. The capital, Salvador, is known for its historic center, Pelourinho, with a rich colonial architecture from the 17th century and located on the Baía de Todos-os-Santos. The city's Carnival brings together costumed revelers who dance to the sound of samba and axé in a huge street party
